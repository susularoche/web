NASTY PIECE OF WORK

AT APPROXIMATELY 15.33 IN LATE MARCH THREE RESIDENTS OF A CUL DE SAC IN MEMPHIS WERE APPROACHED BY A MAN OF, BY ALL ACCOUNTS, UNDISTINGUISHED DESCRIPTION. 'HE REMINDED ME OF AN UNWANTED RELATIVE' ....' A FACE FROM THE BACK OF A MILK CARTON' ... 'HE WAS UNMEMORABLE. A NASTY PIECE OF WORK.' THE VICTIMS CLAIM NO RECOLLECTION OF HIS WORDS OR THE FOLLOWING EVENTS, BUT ALL HAVE USED THE WORD 'POSSESSION' TO DESCRIBE THE EPISODES WHICH FOLLOWED 'I WAS OUT OF MY MIND' SAID THE FIRST VICTIM KNOWN AS AC. ALTHOUGH ALL 3 ACCOUNTS DIFFER IN THEIR QUALITY OF MANIC EPISODE, THEY EACH INDIVIDUALLY SPECIFIED 'HE MADE ME FEEL ALIVE'.

SOUND BY SLAROCHE.

EPISODE 1 OF MEMPHIS CRIME SCENES
