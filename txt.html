CHAOS RULE US IS THE SECOND PUBLICATION FROM WRONG EYE BOOKS. A COLLECTION OF ARTWORKS BY LONDON BASED ARTIST SUSU LAROCHE. BROKEN INTO 3 CHAPTERS, DATING BETWEEN 2008-2020, WITH ACCOMPANYING TEXT BY A CLOSE FRIEND AND COLLABORATOR, CHAOS RULE US GIVES US AN INSIGHT TO THE EVOCATIVE BODY OF WORK LAROCHE HAS DEVELOPED OVER THE PAST DECADE.

100 COPIES, A5, 88 PAGES, IN HAND STITCHED FABRIC SLEEVE
